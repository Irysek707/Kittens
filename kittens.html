Where are my Kittens?

Ten kittens got lost in the city. Will you help finding them?
  • There are total of 10 kittens hiding on the 7x7 board.
  • Simply press the Find the kittens button to start a game.
  • To uncover the field just click on it.
  • Kittens hide in pairs, which means there will be another hiding next to the one you discover.
  • The game ends when you find all kittens or if you uncover 10 empty fields.
